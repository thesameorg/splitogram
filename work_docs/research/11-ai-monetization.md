## AI ideas

- **Receipt scanning** — photo → extract merchant, amount, date, items. Requires an LLM or OCR service.
- **Auto-suggest split** — based on receipt items. Depends on receipt scanning.
- **Natural language entry** — "dinner 45 split with alice and bob." Parse intent → create expense.
- **Auto-categorization** — no categories in current scope, so this is blocked until categories exist (if ever).

## Monetization ideas

- **Premium subscription** — ~$3-5/month via Telegram Stars or USDT
- **Gated features (candidates):** AI scanning, multi-currency within groups, analytics, export
- **Free tier:** unlimited groups, all split types, manual settlement, USDT settlement
- **No ads — ever.**

## Platform expansion ideas

- Multi-currency within groups (per-expense currency override)
- Partial payments ("pay $10 of your $30 debt")
- Analytics dashboard (spending trends)
- CSV/PDF export
- Data export & account deletion (GDPR)
- TON coin as additional settlement currency
