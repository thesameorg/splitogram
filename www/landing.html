How it works

Three steps from shared dinner to cleared debt.

The order matters — track first, simplify automatically, then settle only what's actually owed.

STEP 01

Log the expense

Add what you paid, snap the receipt, pick who's in. Amounts stored in micro-units — no floating-point drift, ever.

STEP 02

Balances simplify themselves

The debt graph collapses to the fewest transfers. Four people, tangled IOUs, one clean number each.

STEP 03

Settle — USDT or offline

Pay with USDT on TON straight from the chat, or mark it settled in cash. Both parties get a notification.

🧮

Why on-chain

The IOU that actually clears.

Shared spreadsheets track debts. They don't collect them. Splitogram closes the loop: the balance you see becomes a real USDT transfer, verified on TON.

  • ✓Stablecoin, not volatility. USDT means $60 owed is $60 paid.
  • ✓Gas estimated live via trace emulation — you attach enough, get the excess back.
  • ✓Failed tx? Auto-rollback. Bounced payments return the balance to open, no stuck states.
  • ✓Prefer cash? Mark it settled offline. Crypto is an option, never a requirement.
